Free spaceship asteroid-dodging game

Astro Coop is a free browser arcade game: fly a spaceship through a starfield and dodge the asteroids for as long as you can. Steer with your mouse, finger, or the arrow keys — no download, no account.

How do you play Astro Coop?

Steer the ship up and down with the mouse, your finger, or the up and down arrows, and avoid the asteroids flying in. Distance is your score and it gets faster over time.

Is Astro Coop free?

Yes — free to play in the browser with no sign-up. Your best flight is saved on your device.

How to play

  1. Steer your ship with the mouse, your finger, or the ↑ / ↓ keys.
  2. Dodge the asteroids flying in from the right. One hit and the flight ends.
  3. Space gets faster the longer you survive — distance is your score.
  4. Beat your best flight and share it. Play as many times as you like.
