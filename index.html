    Free daily games at Roostr

    Roostr is a small collection of free daily games you can play right in your browser — no app, no account, no cost. Each one is a quick, friendly brain-teaser built around real-world facts, with a county-fair twist.

    There are eight to choose from: Ranking (drag five things into order by a hidden measure), Face-Off (call higher or lower four times in a row), Top Pick (tap the biggest or smallest across three rounds), Guess the Flag (name the country from its flag), Guess the Currency (name the country from its money), Coop Run (a quick arcade runner), Astro Coop (a spaceship asteroid dodger), and Memory (a card-matching game). The five daily puzzles refresh every midnight; the arcade games you can play any time.

    What is Roostr?

    Roostr is a collection of free daily browser games with a county-fair theme — five daily brain games (Ranking, Face-Off, Top Pick, Guess the Flag and Guess the Currency) plus arcade games Coop Run, Astro Coop and a card-matching Memory game. A fresh puzzle drops every midnight.

    Is Roostr free to play?

    Yes — every game is completely free, with no sign-up required. Your streaks and stats are saved privately in your own browser.

    How often are new puzzles released?

    The four daily games each get a brand-new puzzle every midnight, your local time. Coop Run, Astro Coop and Memory can be played as many times as you like.

    Do I need an account or an app?

    No. Roostr runs entirely in the browser on phone, tablet or desktop — nothing to install and no account to create.

    How to play

    1. Five contestants enter. Drag them into order by the hidden measure — smallest at the top, largest at the bottom.
    2. Press Judge my order. You get three judgings.
    3. Each card gets a ribbon: right roost one spot off way off
    4. Sweep all five greens to take Best in Show. True values revealed at the end — expect surprises.
