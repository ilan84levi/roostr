    How to play

    1. Five contestants enter. Drag them into order by the hidden measure — smallest at the top, largest at the bottom.
    2. Press Judge my order. You get three judgings.
    3. Each card gets a ribbon: right roost one spot off way off
    4. Sweep all five greens to take Best in Show. True values revealed at the end — expect surprises.

    One puzzle a day. Streaks are sacred.
