Privacy

Last updated: June 2026

Roostr is a small daily game. This page explains, plainly, what happens to data when you play.

What Roostr itself collects

Nothing on a server — Roostr has no accounts and no database of players. Your stats, streak, and today's progress are stored only in your own browser (in localStorage) and never sent anywhere. Clear your browser data and they're gone.

Advertising

Roostr may show ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads, and in some cases to personalise them based on your prior visits to this and other sites. You can review and control this at Google Ads Settings and learn more at Google's advertising policies. If you prefer no ads at all, Roostr Plus removes them.

Coffee / donations

If you choose to "buy me a coffee," the payment is handled entirely by PayPal under PayPal's own privacy policy. Roostr never sees your card details. A donor's name appears on the in-game "Coffee Counter" only if they donated; tell us in the PayPal note if you'd rather stay anonymous, and we'll list you as a kind stranger (or not at all).

Your choices

  • Use an ad blocker, or subscribe to Plus, to avoid ad cookies.
  • Clear your browser storage to erase your local stats.
  • Donations are entirely optional.
