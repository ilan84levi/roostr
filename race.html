Free car racing game — dodge the roosters

Coop Racer is a free browser racing game with a county-fair twist: race down the farm road while roosters hop across it. Steer with two thumbs (left half of the screen turns left, right half turns right), and tilt your phone toward upright to hit the boost. On a computer, use the arrow keys. No download, no account.

How do you play Coop Racer?

Hold your left or right thumb on the screen to steer the car (or use the arrow keys), and dodge the roosters hopping onto the road. Tilt your phone toward upright — or hold the up arrow — to hit the boost. The road speeds up the longer you race, and distance is your score.

How does tilt-to-boost work?

The game reads your phone's orientation: raise it from a flat angle toward upright and the car accelerates. Your browser may ask for motion-sensor permission the first time — that's all it's used for, and nothing leaves your device.

Is Coop Racer free?

Yes — free to play in the browser with no sign-up. Your best race is saved on your device.

How to play

  1. Hold a thumb on the left or right half of the road to steer — or use ← / → on a keyboard.
  2. Roosters hop onto the road. Swerve around them — one bump and the race is over.
  3. Tilt your phone toward upright (or hold ↑) to hit the BOOST — faster road, faster score.
  4. The road speeds up the longer you last. Distance is your score — beat your best and share it.
