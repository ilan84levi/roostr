    Free online currency quiz game

    Roostr Guess the Currency is a free daily currency quiz: name the country from its money across five rounds of four-way multiple choice. Learn the yen, the baht, the złoty and the naira — a quick geography warm-up with new currencies every midnight, no download, no account.

    How do you play the currency quiz?

    A banknote shows a currency's name and symbol, and you pick the country it belongs to from four choices. There are five currencies per day, one guess each.

    Is the currency quiz free?

    Yes — free to play in the browser with no sign-up. Your streak is saved privately on your device.
